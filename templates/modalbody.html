Each guess must be a valid trip on Bratislava's public transit system operated by DP Bratislava. The trip involves services, with transfers possible on stops with the same name, or within a pair of stops a short distance apart. The route will never backtrack and cover a stop more than once, and may only loop at specific points where transfer stops are not possible; for example, at the junction under the SNP bridge.

The route you're guessing is a specific ordered set of services that make the trip. As you make guesses, the backgrounds of the line icons will change color depending on how close your guess is to the actual route.
